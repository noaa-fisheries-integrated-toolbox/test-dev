NOAA Fisheries Integrated Toolbox

Tool: EcoCast (EcoCast)


Authors

Heather Welch, Elliott Hazen, and Kylie Scales

Description

EcoCast is a real-time data tool to help fishers and managers allocate fishing effort to optimize the harvest of target fish while minimizing bycatch of protected species. The EcoCast Map product combines the predicted distributions of target catch species and bycatch species into a single map that suggests better and poorer locations to fish off the US West Coast. The repository linked is to operationalize the tool.
